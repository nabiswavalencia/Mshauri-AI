Inua Jamii Program

Maswali Yanayoulizwa Mara kwa Mara



Hapa kuna majibu ya maswali yanayoulizwa mara kwa mara kuhusu Inua Jamii, mpango wa ulinzi wa kijamii wa Kenya.

Inua Jamii ni nini? Inua Jamii ni mpango wa uhamisho wa pesa kwa wazee na Wakenya wengine walio katika mazingira magumu.

Ninawezaje kupata pesa zangu za Inua Jamii? Unaweza kupata pesa zako kupitia jukwaa la MPESA kwa kupiga *222#. Unaweza pia kutembelea wakala wa MPESA na kitambulisho chako cha kitaifa ili kutoa pesa bila malipo ya muamala.

Ninawezaje kuwasiliana na Inua Jamii? Unaweza kupiga simu Kituo cha Simu cha Inua Jamii kwa nambari 1533 ili kupata usaidizi kuhusu tarehe za malipo, masasisho ya kesi, na makosa ya kuingizwa/kutolewa.

Nani anastahili kujiunga na Inua Jamii? Wazee wenye umri wa miaka 70 na zaidi ambao hawapokei pensheni ya utumishi wa umma wanastahili kujiunga na Inua Jamii.

Inua Jamii inaendeshwaje? Inua Jamii inaendeshwa na Wizara ya Kazi na Ulinzi wa Jamii (MLSP) na Wizara ya Ugatuzi na Maeneo Kame na Nusu Kame (MoDA).

Ninawezaje kujiandikisha kwa Inua Jamii? Unaweza kujiandikisha mwenyewe kwa akaunti kwenye tovuti ya Inuajamii.go.ke. Kurugenzi ya Usaidizi wa Jamii ina haki ya kuidhinisha au kukataa ombi lako.

Frequently asked Questions

Here are some answers to frequently asked questions about Inua Jamii, Kenya's social protection scheme.

What is Inua Jamii? Inua Jamii is a cash transfer program for the elderly and other vulnerable Kenyans.

How do I access my Inua Jamii funds? You can access your funds through the MPESA platform by dialing *222#. You can also visit an MPESA agent with your national ID to withdraw funds without transaction charges.

How can I contact Inua Jamii? You can call the Inua Jamii Call Centre at 1533 for help with payment dates, case updates, and inclusion/exclusion errors.

Who is eligible for Inua Jamii? Older people aged 70 and above who are not receiving a civil service pension are eligible for Inua Jamii.

How is Inua Jamii managed? Inua Jamii is managed by the Ministry of Labour and Social Protection (MLSP) and the Ministry of Devolution and ASAL (MoDA).

How do I register for Inua Jamii? You can self-register for an account on the Inuajamii.go.ke website. The Directorate of Social Assistance has the right to approve or reject your request.
